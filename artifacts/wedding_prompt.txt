DECORATIVE & MOTION DETAILS
────────────────────────────────────────

- Add subtle SVG floral/botanical corner ornaments (line art, gold stroke, no fill) to section dividers and the RSVP section header.
- Add a thin gold horizontal rule (1px, 40% opacity) between major sections.
- Scroll-triggered section entrances: every section fades in + translates up 24px → 0 as it enters the viewport. Use IntersectionObserver.

- Smooth scroll behavior: html { scroll-behavior: smooth; }
- Navigation: fixed top bar, transparent over hero → transitions to ivory/cream with a soft shadow after scrolling 80px. Links: Jost 300, small caps.
